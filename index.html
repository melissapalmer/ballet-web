Schedule

Four key dates between now and curtain up.

  • Fri 1 May 2026 Rehearsal · 08:45 – 13:00

    OLF school hall. Normal ballet outfits. Send snacks, water and a blanket. Moms on duty will look after the girls.

  • Sat 9 May 2026 Rehearsal · 08:45 – 13:30

    OLF school hall. Same drill as 1 May — ballet outfits, snacks, water, blanket.

  • Fri 15 May 2026 Stage rehearsal · sign in 13:00

    Playhouse stage door. All dancers in normal ballet outfits. Sign in at 13:00 — moms take dancers to the dressing rooms. Collection times will be shared closer to the date.

  • Sat 16 May 2026 Show day · 12:00 & 15:00 performances

    Playhouse stage door. Arrive 09:30, sign in — moms take dancers to the dressing rooms. Collection at the stage door after the 15:00 show, around 17:30.

    Backstage rule: only moms on the volunteer roster are allowed backstage on show day.

Venues: rehearsals 1 & 9 May at OLF school hall · 15 & 16 May at the Playhouse Drama Theatre, 231 Anton Lembede Street, Durban.

Dandelion Studio of Dance

Dandelion Studio of Dance trains young dancers across ballet, contemporary and creative movement. The studio’s annual showcase brings the whole company together for a full-length production, giving every dancer — from the youngest beginners to the senior company — the chance to perform on a professional stage.
