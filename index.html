The Show

Dandelion Studio of Dance invites you to step into The Enchanted World of Oz — a vibrant, full-company reimagining of Dorothy’s magical journey, brought to life through the art of dance.

From a quiet Arizona farm, through swirling tornadoes and over the rainbow, audiences will be transported to Munchkin Land and along the Yellow Brick Road, journeying alongside Dorothy, the Scarecrow, the Tin Man and the Lion. Encounter fields of poppies, mischievous crows, flying monkeys, the Wicked Witch and the dazzling Emerald City — all beautifully realised by the talented Dandelion dancers.

This 2026 showcase is a joyful celebration of colour, music and imagination, capturing the courage to dream and the magic of storytelling through movement.

Dandelion Studio of Dance

Dandelion Studio of Dance trains young dancers across ballet, contemporary and creative movement. The studio’s annual showcase brings the whole company together for a full-length production, giving every dancer — from the youngest beginners to the senior company — the chance to perform on a professional stage.
